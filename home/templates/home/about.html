About

This website was created by Adina Shanholtz (Oberlin College '15) for her Fall 2014 private reading. It's intended to allow head cooks in OSCA to search for recipes by main or excess ingredient (for example the co-op just has a ton of Swiss chard no one used) with or without dietary restrictions.
